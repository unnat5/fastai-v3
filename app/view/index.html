Classify Skin Cancer Images

Use images of akiec : Actinic keratoses and intraepithelial carcinoma / Bowen's disease or bcc : basal cell carcinoma or bkl : benign keratosis-like lesions (solar lentigines / seborrheic keratoses and lichen-planus like keratoses or df : dermatofibroma or mel : melanoma or nv : melanocytic nevi or vasc : vascular lesions (angiomas, angiokeratomas, pyogenic granulomas and hemorrhage).
